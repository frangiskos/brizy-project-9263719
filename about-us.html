About us

Welcome to our website

The company Scala City Ads was founded in 2021, starting dynamically its operation from the city of Larnaca.

The newly founded advertising company Scala City Ads, is active in the marketing sector and specifically in the sector of promotion through the massive advertising of products and services of companies that are active in the Cyprus market using as a channel of promotion the outdoor signboards which are placed in prominent locations of Larnaca, where they are visible by the drivers and pedestrians who go past these points throughout the day.

Our service is addressed to businesses of the Cyprus market who are interested to be advertised in outdoor media (outdoor signboards) and for this purpose, our service was created aiming to satisfy the needs of our clients as far as the promotion of the companies, the products and services. Our team is composed by specialized people who know well the role of advertising in the mixture of marketing communication and work with passion and devotion toward this particular objective.

Advertising plays a primary role in the marketing mixture of companies, since it contributes significantly in development and profit.

The outdoor advertising signboards are an effective means of advertising and for this reason more and more businesses choose to be advertised on outdoor signboards, rather than other advertising media and/or include outdoor promotion in combination with other means, in advertising campaigns and become more competitive.

 

Our company, since its very beginning, follows faithfully the concepts of «mission», «vision» and «values» as foundations that determine its position in the market place as far as the services, the responsibilities, and its place towards the people who interact with us and towards the society as a whole.
